    • Mice dropping are initial signs, generally first appeared in your garage, or otherwise the most common places are Kitchen areas; Cupboards, pantries, behind stove or any dark and hidden space in kitchen
    • Physical sighting of mice is sure obvious sign and this could be a fresh mice infestation or a well established mice infestation in your home.
    • You may hear mice scratching in the walls, celling while mostly lower floor of the house. Some time at early stage of mice infestation scratching or chewing sounds common, either mice making a hole in creating route or establishing nest around.
